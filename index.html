BRICK BREAKER MISSION


# Overview

Breakout is a traditional arcade game developed and published by Atari, Inc. The BRICK BREAKER MISSION is a small implementation of this famous ‘Breakout’ game.


# How to play

In this game, we have layers of bricks and a ball to break the bricks. The player moves the paddle from left to right using keyboard to keep the ball from falling. A life is used when the player fails to hit the ball. A player has a maximum of 3 lives. A brick disappears when it’s hit by the ball. At the same time, there will be music outputs to match different game effects. There is a scoring system that reflects how efficient the user is to clear the bricks. Number of total paddle hits and maximum brick hits per trip are two of the possible factors.
